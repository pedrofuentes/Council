---
title: Direct message experts (@mentions)
description: Route messages to specific experts in panel chat using @mentions.
---

In panel chat, prefix your message with `@slug` to route it to one or more specific experts.

## Mention a single expert

```
You> @strategist What's our biggest competitive moat?

[Sarah Chen — VP Strategy]
Our data network effects are the primary moat...
```

Only the mentioned expert responds — others stay silent for that turn.

## Mention multiple experts

```
You> @strategist @devils-advocate Is our pricing strategy sustainable?

[Sarah Chen — VP Strategy]
The current pricing model aligns with our value metric...

[Marcus Cole — Devil's Advocate]
I'd push back on that. Three of our five largest accounts have asked for
volume discounts...
```

## Important constraints

**Mentions must appear at the start of your message:**

- ✅ `@strategist What do you think?` — works
- ❌ `What do you think @strategist?` — treated as a general message to everyone

## Error handling

If you mention a slug that isn't in the panel, Council shows an error listing the available experts — no guessing required.
