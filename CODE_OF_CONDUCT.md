# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as
contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in the Council
project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background or
identity. We are committed to acting and interacting in ways that contribute to
an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

This project adopts the **[Contributor Covenant, version 2.1][covenant]** as its
Code of Conduct. Please read the canonical text for the full set of expected
behaviors, our responsibilities, scope, and enforcement guidelines.

[covenant]: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported privately to the maintainer **[@pedrofuentes](https://github.com/pedrofuentes)**
by opening a private [GitHub Security Advisory][report] on this repository. All
complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly, and reporter
privacy and security will be respected.

[report]: https://github.com/pedrofuentes/Council/security/advisories/new
